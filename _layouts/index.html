About Me

I'm a Ph.D candidate of the Institute For Advanced Study (IAS), Tsinghua University; an intern in Microsoft Research Asia (MSRA).

Education

I got my B.S. in Tsinghua University in 2013. I majored in physics during my undergraduate study and did some research in astrophysics. My first seminar project was about the planet migration. Under the supervision of Prof. Douglas Lin, I explained part of the reasons of the resonance between the planets in the same solar system. Another project I focused on was the gas depletion of Hot Jupiters. My bachelor's thesis is about fitting the light curves of supernovae Ia, which is conducted under the guidance of Prof. Xiaofeng Wang.
After I graduated, I became a Ph.D candidate in IAS, THU. In 2014, I decided to turn my research realm into computer science. Since then, I've been researching in the field of computer vision and deep learning. Currently, I'm working on projects about photo selection.

Interests

I have quite a lot of hobbies, like soccer, reading, Chinese poems. Moreover, I'm always eager to experience new things and find new life styles.

Soccer:
I served as a member of the department soccer team and was the captain of it from 2011.04 to 2013.06. From 2014.08, I joined another soccer team called Lingpao Tsinghua, members of which are all alumni of THU. I'm a soccer fan of Arsenal which is a club in the Premier League.

Chinese poems:
Tang poetry and Song poetry are both great treasure for the Chinese. I learned some rules and forms of classical poetic composition in 2010. Sometimes I write some Chinese poems, though not good ones. I'll post my poems in the blog, of course in Chinese.
